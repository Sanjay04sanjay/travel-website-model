DUBAI UAE

DUBAI is famous for its iconic landmarks, including the Burj Khalifa, the world’s tallest building;
Palm Jumeirah, an artificial island; and the luxurious Burj Al Arab hotel.
It’s also known for hosting large-scale events such as the Dubai Shopping Festival and Expo 2020.

Shopping:

Dubai is renowned for its massive malls, such as The Dubai Mall, one of the largest in the world,
featuring indoor ice skating, an aquarium, and endless luxury brands.
The Mall of the Emirates is also famous for its indoor ski resort.

Culture:

Although Dubai is modern and cosmopolitan, it maintains its traditional Arabian culture.
The Dubai Museum in the Al Fahidi Fort offers insight into its history,
while local markets, or souks, like the Gold and Spice Souks,
give a glimpse of the old trading lifestyle.
